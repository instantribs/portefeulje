About Orangeway Farms

For over a century, Orangeway Farms has grown oranges with patience, care, and respect for the land. From a small riverside orchard to a trusted regional farm, our focus has always stayed the same: quality fruit and sustainable farming.

Who We Are

Take a second to see Orangeway Farms as we see it — a place of growth, care, and peace.

Our Story

Orangeway Farms began in 1912 when Elias Hathaway planted the first orange trees beside a shallow riverbend. The early years were difficult, with droughts, uneven harvests, and limited equipment, but the orchard slowly took root through persistence and careful cultivation.

Over the decades, the farm expanded steadily. Improved irrigation, modern packing facilities, and sustainable growing methods helped Orangeway become known for dependable, high-quality fruit across the region.

Today, the farm blends old and new — preserving original orchard rows while using modern systems to grow responsibly for future generations.

Our Mission

We believe farming should be sustainable, thoughtful, and built for the long term. Every decision — from irrigation to harvesting — is made with future generations in mind.

Orangeway Farms remains proudly hands-on. We combine traditional farming values with practical innovation to produce fruit that customers can enjoy year after year.
